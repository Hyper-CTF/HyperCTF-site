What is a CTF
A CTF, or capture-the-flag is a cybersecurity and computer science competition intended as practice to real life cyber-warfare scenarios that could potentially occur in the real world. CTF cybersecurity competitions are run around the world every day for many different reasons. Some of them are for fun, some for company recruitment, and some for education.





The Challenges
The challenges in HyperCTF range from easy to hard so even if you don't know anything about cybersecurity you can still play and learn. HyperCTF challenges are also created with the players who have competed before in mind. Because of this we have created some very challenging challenges





The Team
The HyperCTF team consists of 3 high school students who wish to give aspiring cybersecurity engineers the tools necessary to be able to go into the field. To do this they have created HyperCTF.
